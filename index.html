Security

🔐

Your credentials stay with your bank.

CardAlerts connects through Plaid — you sign in on your bank's own page, and we receive only read-only transaction data. Access tokens are encrypted with AWS Key Management Service. We never sell your data, and notification history is deleted after 90 days. CardAlerts cannot move money, block charges, or change anything in your accounts.

FAQ

Fair questions

Does CardAlerts have access to my money?

No. CardAlerts is read-only. We can see transaction descriptions and balances — we cannot move money, block charges, or make changes to your accounts.

Which banks are supported?

Over 12,000 US institutions via Plaid, including Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, Capital One, Citi, and most credit unions.

How fast are the alerts?

Usually within seconds of your bank reporting the transaction. Exact speed varies by institution.

Can I stop SMS messages?

Yes — reply STOP to any message, or turn SMS off in the app. Message and data rates may apply; frequency varies with your card activity.

How do I delete my data?

Account Settings → Delete Account. All bank connections are revoked immediately and your data is deleted within 30 days.
